8 Things You Didnt Know About Tom Brittney

Publish date: 2024-08-03

➡ Tom Brittney Biography

Tom Brittney (born 26 October 1990) is a British actor best known for his role as Reverend Will Davenport in Grantchester, which he has played since series 4. His acting abilities have piqued the interest of the audience, as he has excelled in each role. He has put in a lot of effort to gain popularity among fans. And those fans will now be interested in learning some fun facts about him that we’ve compiled

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Tom Brittney

1. Atheist

Despite the fact that actor Tom Brittney appears to be born with a backward collar and a pious calling, he is an atheist. During one of his interviews, he revealed this. [1]

2. Quarantine Chef

During the time of quarantine, the actor also tried cooking and posted a video story on his Instagram account. [2]

3. Pet Lover

He has also a pet dog in his house whom he really loves. He also keeps posting cute and adorable pictures with his pet on Instagram.

4. Suffered From Body Dysmorphia

He’s suffered from body dysmorphia. “I used to take off days from school because I just hated the way I looked,” he said in 2020. I’m self-conscious about how I look, and I’ll berate myself if I don’t perform well. [3]

5. Photographer

Tom has excellent photography skills, as evidenced by his Instagram posts. He is obsessed with photography. We can conclude from his Instagram that if he weren’t an actor, he would be a photographer.

6. Cricket Fan

As almost every person is a fan of cricket, Tom is also one of them and he loves to watch cricket. His Instagram shows that he has gone to watch many cricket matches in the stadium.

7. Replacement Of “James Norton”

He replaced “James Norton” in his current movie “Grandchester”. Directors and producers had considered James for that role but later ended up casting Tom.

8. Dream role

James Bond is his ideal role. Brittney began his acting career in films because they “inspired him with their wonderful format.” And, like many others in the industry, he has his sights set on the biggest job in the industry: succeeding Daniel Craig. “I’d love to play Bond,” he says. “I’ve always been a fan of those films,” Brittney said.
