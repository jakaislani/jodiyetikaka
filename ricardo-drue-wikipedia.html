After the death of Ricardo Drue, a famous Antiguan singer and musical artist, fans are now curious about his personal details and are searching for his Wikipedia page. Let’s learn the history of the Antiguan singer.

Ricardo Drue was an R&B singer and songwriter who was born in the year 1985 on July 31st.

Except for singing, he was also talented and interested in playing guitar, piano, and drums. 

He collaborated with famous artists like Sean Kingston, Skinny Fabulous, and Patrice Roberts in his musical career.

After his debut as an R&B artist in the year 2015, Ricardo was able to get himself the Best New Artist award at the COTT Awards.

Sadly, the death news of Ricardo Drue on 12th, 2023, made people curious about his personal life as they were searching for his Wikipedia page.

Ricardo Drue Wikipedia: Musical Genius From Antigua With A Tragic End At A Young Age

Ricardo comes from the city of Antigua. He was born there and grew up with his four siblings.

He was not the only one famous in his family; Ricardo’s mother, Nicola Barriteau, is also a famous television personality.

His journey in music began at the age of five when his parents put him in various musical trainings for piano, guitar, and drums.

Most people, including Ricardo, knew music was his true calling.

However, he chose to study business management for his higher education.

Finally, Ricardo received his bachelor’s degree in business management from the University of West Indies.

While studying at the university, he joined Dance and Vocal Quartet Crossovah in 2004.

The audience loved seeing Ricardo perform live, and consequently, the band gained huge popularity and started performing at various events and festivals.

Sadly, the audience will never experience his live performances as he is not with us anymore.

Ricardo Drue was only 38 years old at the time of his death.

After being in the band for a while, Ricardo became a solo artist. He released his first solo song, Vagabond, which became a huge hit.

He describes his song Vagabond producer, Mr. Roots, as a very talented and supportive man. 

Ricardo’s music career was going perfectly, and he was also getting the public’s attention.
