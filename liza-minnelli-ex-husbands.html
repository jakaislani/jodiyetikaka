Meet David, Mark, Jack and Peter

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Liza Minnelli first and foremost is an American singer and performer who has carved a legacy for herself in entertainment.

She was born on March 12, 1946, in Hollywood, California, to famous parents Judy Garland and Vincente Minnelli, a film director.

She went at the High School of Performing Arts and Chadwick School in New York City. She spent a year at Scarsdale High School before acting in the play “The Diary of Anne Frank.”

She spent her childhood going to studios, sound stages, and seeing movies being made. She had her debut film appearance at the age of three, with her mother, in the 1949 musical ‘In the Good Old Summertime.’

In 1972, she received critical praise for her performance as Sally Bowles, a floundering nightclub singer, in the musical “Cabaret.” She received the Academy Award and the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role for her performance.

Her personal life was full of ups and downs. We take a look at some of her ex-husbands;

  • David Gest

Gest was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, to a Jewish family.

Gest married Liza Minnelli on March 16, 2002, and the couple divorced in July 2003.
He sued her for $10 million in October 2003, saying she was aggressive and physically abusive during their marriage, which the affidavit blamed on Minnelli’s long-term alcoholism.  

Minnelli vehemently refuted the allegations, stating that Gest was only interested in her  money. In September 2006, the lawsuit was dismissed due to a lack of a triable question offact.

In 1979, she married sculptor and theatre manager Mark Geo. During this marriage, she had three miscarriages. Minelli desired to create a family with artist Mark Gero.

Although there isn’t much information regarding their relationship, Minnelli has spoken candidly about the difficult times of this marriage.

In 1992, the couple divorced.

Minnelli went on to Jack Hayley Jr. in 1974. Judy Minelli co-starred in The Wizard Of Oz alongside Hayley Jr’s father, Jack Hayley, as the Tin Man.

The marriage ended after five years because the couple was more platonic than amorous. “Haley was spouse number two and straight,” the Guardian noted, “but they were friends rather than lovers.”

Minnelli discovered Allen having an affair with a man on the night of their wedding.
