Is Better Call Saul A Prequel Or Sequel To Breaking Bad? Season 6 Episodes Review And Cast Details

Publish date: 2024-09-10

Better Call Saul is a popular show created by AMC television series, it is considered a phenomenal series that has lived up to the hype of Breaking Bad. 

The series is created by Vince Gilligan and Peter Gould and stars the slimy lawyer from the Breaking Bad Jimmy McGill/Saul Goodman played by Bob Odenkirk.

The series plays with the characters introduced in the breaking bad and showcases the navigation of the law by a crooked lawyer in order to make name for himself in terms of power, money, and fame.

Is Better Call Saul A Prequel Or Sequel to Breaking Bad? Timeline Explained

Better Call Saul is the prequel to Breaking bad, it is prior to the rise of Heisenberg aka Walter White. Better Call Saul showcases Saul Goodman’s rise as a lawyer.

Initially, Saul does not even have a license he secures his law license through an online law course and works as a lawyer in his own small firm in seasons one and two. However, his brother Chuck sabotages his work and Saul/Jimmy does vice versa.

Jimmy represents the street-level gangs with his partner Kim which lead him to meet the new leader of the Salamanca clan, Lalo Salamanca. Mike a badass operative is struggling with his faith but nonetheless joins Gus who is another mafia, as a full-time operative.

Moreover, breaking bad starts with a chemistry teacher Walter White trying to make money for his family as he finds out that he is suffering from lung cancer.

Walter partners with his high school student Jesse Pinkman in order to make meth and sell them. Their illegal activity is an empire built with the help of Gus by overcoming various conflicts.

Hence, with the connection between Mike, Saul finds out about Walt and offers his services to launder money, and consults Walt about his problems.

How Many Episodes Are There In Better Call Saul Season 6? Review

The season is set to release on both AMC and Netflix simultaneously. There are a total of 13 episodes that are divided into two parts one part shall release from 19th April till 23rd May of this year.

The first and second episodes will release on the same day and the rest will be released every week on Monday. Similarly, the final six episodes shall be released by 12th July 2022, likely to be released in the same format.

Furthermore, the final batch of the show is likely to be a banger as Jimmy and Kim had barely survived the rival gangs’ war, and they are undeterred. Likewise, there is likely to be a war between Nacho the agent of Gus, and Lola Salamanca due to an unsuccessful attempt of assassination in his life by Nacho.

Ultimately, the series shall bring a mix of dark comedy with the action-packed drama, that makes the viewers keep their focus only on the screen. Also, the addition of an exceptional camera and VFX team shall bring a beginning to the end of an exceptional show.

Better Call Saul Season 6 Cast: New And Returning

The cast members returning to the show are Lavell Crawford as Huell Babineaux, an associate of Saul, Dennis Boutsikaris as Rich Schweikart, a partner at Schweikart & Cokely, and Ed Begley Jr. as Clifford Main, managing partner at Davis & Main.

Likewise, Steven Bauer as Don Eladio Vuente, head of the Juarez drug cartel, and Javier Grajeda as Juan Bolsa, member of the Juarez drug cartel.

Therefore, the addition to the cast is likely to be the possibility of the appearances of Brayan Cranston and Aaron Paul as Walter White and Jesse Pinkman.
