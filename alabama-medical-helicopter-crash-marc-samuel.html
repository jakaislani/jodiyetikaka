Tragically, a medical helicopter crashed in Alabama on Sunday evening while responding to an emergency call.

According to officials, the crash resulted in the loss of two crew members, leaving a third with serious injuries.

The incident happened around 5:20 pm when the Life Saver helicopter, which Air Methods operated, was attempting to land.

During the crash, the helicopter was on a mission to transport a hiker experiencing breathing difficulties and chest pains.

However, fortunately, there was no patient on board during the incident. The cause of the accident is not yet known, and an investigation will be carried out to determine the cause of the crash.
