Publish date: 2024-05-13

The first trailer for Daniel Radcliffe's new film Imperium, which sees him play an FBI agent that infiltrates a group of neo-Nazis, has finally been released.

Radcliffe stars as Nate Foster, a youthful FBI analyst who is tasked with going undercover inside a vicious far right-wing terrorist group planning to use a dirty bomb. The film takes its inspiration from the real-life story of FBI agent Michael German, who also helped co-write the script.
