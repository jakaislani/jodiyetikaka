What is Joel Dahmen's net worth?

Publish date: 2024-09-12

JOEL DAHMEN is one of America's most consistent golfers to touch the green - and he has earned a few dollars in doing so.

Dahmen, 35 secured his first PGA Tour triumph in 2021 at the Corales Puntacana Resort.

And the American idol is the talk of the town once again having landed himself an iconic episode on the new Netflix documentary, 'Full Swing'.

Dahmen has enjoyed a number of good results during his playing career.

But how much money has his accomplishments made him over the years?

What is Joel Dahmen's net worth?

Joel Dahmen's net worth is reportedly in the region of $11million (£9m).

This figure sees the American currently sit inside the top 200 on the PGA Tour career money list.

Dahmen has averaged over $1m a season since 2017-18 with his best campaign coming in the year 2019-20 where he collected $2.1m.

And Dahmen has also claimed multiple 10s in the PGA Championship and US Open netting over £330,000.

However, the golfing star's biggest payday came in his Puntacana Championship victory when he took home £495,000.
