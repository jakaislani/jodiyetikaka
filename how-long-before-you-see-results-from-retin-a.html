How long before you see results from Retin-A?

Publish date: 2024-05-13
You may notice tretinoin starting to work within 2 to 3 weeks, but it can take 6 weeks or more to experience the full benefit. If you don't see improvement within 12 weeks, or if you have significant improvement and wonder if you should start using it less frequently, talk to your doctor.

How long does it take to see the effects of Retin-A?

It might take two or three weeks of using Retin-A (treitinoin) to see any benefits. However, in some cases, it may take six weeks or more.

What to expect when starting Retin-A?

2-4 Weeks. It's not uncommon to experience slight irritation like peeling and dryness or sensitivity as your skin adjusts. If you're prone to acne, you may also experience “purging”, which is when existing acne buildup surfaces faster due to increased cell turnover.

How long do you purge with Retin-A?

You can expect the tretinoin purge to start soon after your first application, usually within the first two weeks. However, the timeframe can vary.
