Slim Jxmmi Allegedly Attacked His Pregnant Ex-Girlfriend

Publish date: 2024-08-04

A relationship is a fluctuating thing. Sometimes you enclose a strong connection with your loved ones, and sometimes you will experience a bad experience with them. But domestic violence can never be included in the subsidiaries of fluctuating components that relationships go through.

And the American rapper, Slim Jxmmi, has gone beyond the limit. He allegedly attacked his pregnant ex-girlfriend named Kee physically, breaking her tooth in the process. Sources claim that Kee is six months pregnant with Jxmmi's child; however, she is not treated as a pregnant woman should be. Rapper Jxmmi allegedly punched her on the mouth, which broke her teeth and the bone in her gums.

Reports say that Jxmmi paid money to Kee to get veneers to get her tooth maintained. Despite the domestic violence Kee is suffering, she has not pressed any charges against Jxmmi due to his fear.

It has come to the acknowledgment the recent phase of Slim Jxmmi and Kee's relationship has been physical. Sources suggested that Kee has been Jxmmi's 'punching bag,' which happened after the death of his father earlier this year.

As of now, Kee is safe and sound, and she has called her relationship off with rapper Slim Jxmmi. Also, she plans to stay out of Jxmmi's reach after she gives birth to the rapper's children. On the other hand, Jxmmi is the father of a son, whose identity has been kept private. He first talked about his child back in 2018 on the Breakfast Club.

He mentioned, 'You could be going down a bad road, you know what I'm sayin'? You could have a kid; it could make you change the whole way you look at everything.' 'For me, I like having a son. I just had my first son, I like the responsibility... My momma likes it, she likes having a grandson,' he added.

How Much Is Slim Jxmmi's Net Worth?

Slim Jxmmi garners his net worth the professional career as a rapper and musician. As of now, he has a fortune of $9 million. Slim Jxmmi is a founder of the duo band called Rae Sremmund, alongside his brother named Swae Lee. They formed the duo back in 2009 and have been contributing their credits in the entertainment industry.

Since their establishment, Rae Sremmund has brought four albums, namely, SremmLife (2015), SremmLife 2 (2016), SR3MM (2018), and SremmLife 4 (2020). Their debut album SremmLife reached no. 1 on the US Rap and US R&B/Hip-Hop charts. Similarly, it reached #5 on the Billboard 200 chart.

Likewise, their second album entitled 'SremmLife 2' topped on #2 on the US Rap chart, #3 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart, and #4 on the Billboard 200 chart. The duo is widely-famous for their hit songs like Black Beatles, Come Get Her, Powerglide, Real Chill, Bedtime Stories, Keep God First, and many others.
