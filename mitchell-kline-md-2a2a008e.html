Dr. Mitchell Kline, MD New York, NY

Publish date: 2024-05-22

Dr. Mitchell Kline is a dermatologist in New York, NY and teaches as a clinical Asst professor at NewYork-Presbyterian/Weill Cornell. He received his medical degree from the University of Pennsylvania and trained there in Internal Medicine and has been in practice 32 years. He also speaks multiple languages, including French. He specializes in Melanoma diagnostics and surgery, digital skin exams with non-invasive diagnosis via confocal microscopy, cosmetic dermatology and dermatologic surgery and is highly experienced in dermatologic surgery, malignant melanoma, cosmetic dermatology, mohs micrographic surgery, and melanoma and cutaneous malignancies.
