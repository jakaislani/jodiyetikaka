Skinny Chocolate Raspberry Icebox Cake

Because summer is too short to not eat cake...Keep the oven off and enjoy this Skinny Chocolate Raspberry Icebox Cake today! This easy dessert recipe is perfect for hot summer days AND swimsuit season! Comes together in no time and trust me, it will be the star of the show!

Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword icebox cake, skinny chocolate raspberry icebox cake Prep Time 30 minutes minutes Total Time 30 minutes minutes Servings 15 servings Calories 341kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 50 full sheets of graham crackers

Chocolate Pudding Layers

  • 2.8 oz instant chocolate pudding mix sugar free, fat free
  • 3 Weight Watchers® GIANT Chocolate Fudge Ice Cream Bars melted (about 1 cup melted low-fat ice cream)
  • 3 cups cold non-fat milk

Cheesecake Layer

  • 8 oz light cream cheese softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla or almond extract
  • 8 oz lite whipped topping

Additional ingredients

  • 1 pint fresh raspberries
  • 8 oz lite whipped topping
  • chocolate shavings

Instructions

Chocolate Pudding Layers

  • Beat pudding mix, melted ice cream, and milk together for about two minutes. Refrigerate pudding for at least 30 minutes before continuing on.

Cheesecake Layer

  • Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy, about three minutes. Beat in vanilla extract.

  • Fold in whipped topping. Set aside.

Assembly

  • Line a 9 x 13 inch baking dish with graham crackers. Break up as you need to. I used a little less than 10 full sheets per layer.

  • Spread half of the pudding mixture on top of the graham crackers.

  • Place another layer of graham crackers on top of the pudding.

  • Spread cheesecake layer on top of the graham crackers.

  • Place another layer of graham crackers on top of the cheesecake.

  • Spread remaining half of the pudding mixture on top of the graham crackers.

  • Place another layer of graham crackers on top of the pudding.

  • Spread remaining 8 oz container of lite whipped topping on top of the graham crackers.

  • Top with raspberries and chocolate shavings.

  • Refrigerate until ready to serve.
