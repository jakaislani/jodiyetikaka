Eliza J. Levandoski Car Accident; 16 Year Old Boy, Eliza J. Levandoski Passes In a Fatal Accident

The fatal accident that happened on Saturday near Ellsworth has an official victim named by McLean County Coroner Dr. Kathy Yoder. The 16-year-old girl who perished in the accident was Eliza J. Levandoski, who was from Arrowsmith, Illinois.

Levandoski was declared dead at the crash scene, which is situated at 2850 East at 1200 North Road in Ellsworth, according to the reports. According to the autopsy’s preliminary results, she tragically died from serious head injuries sustained in an automobile-semi-truck and trailer collision. According to the allegations, she was wearing a seatbelt and driving the car when it crashed. Toxicology tests are still pending to identify any other variables, though.

In an effort to learn more and bring comfort to the bereaved family and community, the McLean County Sheriff’s Department and the Coroner’s Office are actively looking into the facts surrounding this tragic tragedy.
